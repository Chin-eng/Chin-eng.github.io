Meeting Requirements

  • I schedule meetings between 8:30AM-4PM (no meetings after 4PM);
  • 1:1 meetings should be (max) 30 minutes.
  • All meetings should have a pre-sent agenda (even if just a bullet point list of key talking points).
  • To optimize work efficiency, I prefer meetings in batches (back-to-back).
